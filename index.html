Instructions for the to do app exercise

My To Do App!

    My tasks:

Add a task!

Part I - Requirements:

  • Add a new "to do" item by submitting a HTML form.

  • Mark a "to do" item as completed by crossing out the text of the "to do" item from the list.

  • Remove a "to do" item from the list.

Part II - Additional Requirements

  • Now that the part I requirements are met and the app is functioning per Part I requirements add a localstorage feature where the user can save their "to do" items in localStorage.

  • Make sure that when the page refreshes, the todos on the page remain there.
